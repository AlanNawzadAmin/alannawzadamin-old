Alan Nawzad Amin

Faculty Fellow at NYU Courant Institute, working with the Wilson Lab. I work on statistical models built on modern, large databases of biological sequences. My projects either build models that can leverage these datasets in new ways, or seek to understand why these models work. In the summer of 2023, I was a postdoc at Jura bio. I completed my PhD in the Harvard Systems Biology program supervised by Debora Marks in 2023. I graduated with a BS in Biochemistry and Mathematics from the University of Toronto in 2019.
